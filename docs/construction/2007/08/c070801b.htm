1. Announcement Inserts

1.1 Area Undergoing Repaving

nfoWare> repaving> 2007-08-04 announcement>


nfoWare Is Being Repaved.

Migration to a new server introduced a number of problems that require repairs.  In addition to those defects, some older pages also need to be modified and reorganized for consistency across all of nfoWare.  While the site is being repaved to accomplish both kinds of improvements, you may notice discrepancies and problems with links to site content.

Check the Site Repaving Project pages for additional details on the known defects and the approach to correction and upgrade.  Contact the nfoWare author with questions and to report any defects that you notice.

 

The basic notice is used for notification in all locations subject to the repaving project.  As the project moves through different stages, this notice may be modified accordingly so that there is a link to the description of the then-current stage.

2. Current Activity 

  1. The first priority is to have the construction structure of the site be current and properly connected. 

    • This is accomplished by performing a review starting with the "front porch:" the root materials at http://nfoWare.com/.  Work items and their completion will be reported as part of the front-porch Construction Zone.
         

    • After the front porch is adjusted, the construction structure of each subfolder is reviewed, extending recursively through all of the construction-structure elements only.    Additional items that happen to be noticed will be added to the work items of the applicable construction zone. 
        

    • The construction structure and the related construction zones can be located by navigating the construction structure provided at the front porch.
        

    • When the construction structure is reviewed and adjusted as necessary, a completion message will be inserted to avoid wondering which structures have and have not been reviewed.
        

  2. The second priority is to have the Toolcraft content be consistent, reachable, and properly linked. 

    • The modifications to this compilation of material will begin with those important for the ODMJNI Public Beta.  This allows compilation of general information on the use of Visual C++ 2005 Express Edition, the Java 1.5 Development Kit, and related Windows lore that is relied upon for ActiveODMA projects.
        

    • The Toolcraft section has a construction zone for management of its internal construction structure.  Items about elements of the overall repaving will be found here.
        

    • The Toolcraft section also has a Catalog and a Job Jar for the collection of toolNotes.  Any repaving at the collection level will be reflected in that job jar.  This level will provide a checklist for the review of individual toolNotes for any adjustment and repaving.
         

    • The individual toolNotes also have their own Diary & Job Jar pages.  In addition to regular work items and notes, those pages may also carry detail items that apply to repaving of the individual note. 
        

    • As appropriate, completion messages will be inserted to avoid wondering which toolNotes have and have not been reviewed.
         

  3. The third priority is to have the Construction Material content be updated.  The Construction Material construction structure and its content needs to be revamped as first-class support for nfoWare.
       

  4. These activities will be carried out in parallel so that the essential needs to provide resources to the ODMJNI Public Beta and the posting of Cybersmith and VC++ Novice guidance are met as quickly as possible.  (Note that Cybersmith articles may also be appropriately posted to TROSTing.org)
      

  5. On completion of these adjustments, other sections will be reviewed and repaved, including the construction section and others that have content other than construction structure.  Since nfoWare is one of the newest parts of  nfoCentrale, the changes here will be relatively straightforward.
