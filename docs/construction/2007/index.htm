Immediate Structure

  • 08: nfoWare Construction Notes for August, 2007

Notes are organized in a hierarchy of months within year, and then numbered sequentially within a month.  Month folders are not created until preparing to initiate a construction note in that month.

The only reliable way to access these notes is using the catalog.
