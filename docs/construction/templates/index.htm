This section holds template-material subfolders.  The templates are applicable to the construction of web pages and construction notes.  Templates have folio pages in the block c0000xx.  By convention, c000000 and c000001 are reserved for the Construction Material Job Jar & Diary and the Construction Material Catalog.

For further information, refer to Template entries in

  • c000001: NuovoDoc Construction Materials Catalog

The catalog provides status and links to all construction materials, including the available template materials.
