Exemplar Customization

hybridForm is a template and exemplar for repurposing as miser project web topic sections with their own article subsections.  This current Article Diary & Job Jar page is the exemplar for topic/ Construction Zones.  The practice is to have the topic/c000000.htm and construction.htm always provide a Construction Zone for the introduced topic/.  This c000000.htm exemplar provides initial checklists of work items and additional ones that arise in establishment of a customized topic/ subdirectory of web pages.  If there is to be no such (preserved) Construction Zone, this material should be deleted and also removed from the immediate infrastructure on the topic/index.htm page after customization is completed.  The portable practice is to keep things familiar and always have the Construction Zone present.  Usage will require employment of an HTML editor.  Customization is as follows.

  1. In the top banner, the site/project construction image should already appear correct in the left cell.  The link should be to "../", the project home directory.
  2. In the title section of the top banner, the term "hybridForm:" should be replaced by the topic name. 
  3. The Page Properties title should also be adjusted in the same manner.
  4. In the far right block of the top banner, without altering the links, change the topic name from "hybridForm" to the name of the new topic directory..  The link should remain "./".  The "c000000" link should be to "c000000.htm".
  5. Adjust the Revision History to reflect the initialization of this page as topic/c000000.htm.  Either continue the version numbering or reset to an 0.0.0 that reflects the hybridForm origin.  Note that there is an anchor on the revision numbers.  Use that pattern.  In the top banner, link the version number to the one you are having as the latest and correct the link text to match.  
  6. No changes should be required in the footer banner except for the "created" entry and possible identity and link to the author.  Verify that there are no mistaken links in that banner.
  7. In the body of the table, clear/delete any rows you do not wish to preserve or rely upon.  See existing Construction Zones for a common approach to how "Status" entries are made and how completed items are shaded and moved to the lower part of the list where completed items are accumulated for historical purposes.  The entries with "exemplar" are here for carrying out when repurposing this page.  There may be additional ones of your own, but the "exemplar" entries do provide a checklist that may be useful (in item 10, below.).
  8. The tracking of paving/repaving/repurposing notification at the top should reflect the case that will apply when this page's customization is complete.  If that is not aligned with the current situation, adjust to employ the correct notification, if any.
  9. When the customization of this page is complete, delete this complete "Exemplar Customization" section and commit this repurposed version to the repository topic/ folder.
  10. Now work through the exemplar work items in the table of this page.  Delete them or complete them, and continue for Construction Zone purposes under the newly-established topic.
